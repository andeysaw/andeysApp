Welcome to my site! My favorite way to describe my experiences is with pictures. As a result, I try to make photographs the main focus of my site. I am an avid explorer, and love to travel to new places or help others discover places I have visited. Usually I choose to go to the great outdoors, but occasionally seek out other interesting sights.

This is also a place where I try out different web technologies. The current iteration of this site uses AngularJS, Foundation, PHP, and MySQL. I also make use of GulpJS, Modernizr, JQuery, and Font Awesome. The site may change whenever I make the time to work on it.

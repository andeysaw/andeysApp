We checked out of our hotel, then got a taxi to the airport, catching our China Airlines flight on time. I was a bit concerned that we might be delayed by the heavy rain, but it made no difference to our schedule. In a short time, we reached Taipei, Taiwan then continued on to Los Angeles.

We both got off the plane and had no problems with customs and immigration, then I parted with Alexander. I then took a domestic flight to Las Vegas McCarran International Airport and rode a shuttle to Luxor to meet up with my family. The intense dry desert air of Las Vegas felt markedly different from Hong Kong's humidity.

The international travel was now over and I relaxed and did laundry. In the next few days, I would meet with friends and explore Las Vegas, finally going home via Oakland Aiport on July 6.

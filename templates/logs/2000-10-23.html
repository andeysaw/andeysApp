At dawn, I finally left Jiranan's place in Horsham, taking the train to Gatwick Airport. After checking in, I boarded the Virgin Atlantic Airways flight bound for San Francisco, California. I tracked the flight, and had particularly interesting views outside my window when the plane flew over Manitoba, Canada and Montana in the United States.

Before landing, I got a great view of San Francisco, California. I breezed through customs and immigration, then took the bus and train back to my place in Berkeley, unpacked, and relaxed.

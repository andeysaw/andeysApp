My Photography

I compose and take my pictures as I go, more interested in exploring rather than waiting for the best light conditions. Also, I like to travel light, especially when hiking strenuously. As a result, I have taken many of my pictures with a point-and-shoot camera. I usually don't spend any longer than a minute to compose a shot, but always keep an eye on what may make a good photograph. By default, photos are shown in a pseudo-random order.
